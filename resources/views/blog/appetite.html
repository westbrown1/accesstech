The Best Ways To Curb Your Appetite (Brushing Your Teeth Is One Way)

Brushing Your Teeth With Mint Toothpaste Will Curb Your Appetite.



I was hungry between meals today and researched the best way to curb my appetite. When I clicked on Wikihow, I discovered that brushing my teeth with a mint toothpaste should curb my appetite. And guess what, it did! Now I have a ferocious appetite. I'm pretty sure I could be a professional eater. You know, one those people who could eat a thousand hot dogs in 10 minutes. Well maybe not a thousand, but a lot of hot dogs. Anyway, if you want to lose some weight, eat a reasonable portion of healthy food, then stop and go brush your teeth. You might be surprised at what the mint flavored toothpaste can do for your appetite, and your breath!

Take a toothbrush and tube to work with you. If you're going out to eat at a restaurant. Or going to a Super Bowl party. Take a miny tube and toothbrush with you. After eating a sensible portion of food, excuse yourself to the restroom and have a quick brush. Be sure to do it before dessert time...

If you must snack, choose a volumnous food, popcorn is perfect to help you feel full between or after meals.

A bonus tip, if you cannot get over your desire for a Big Mac and large fry. Stop. Take time to search "Nutrition in a Big Mac". Then check out the fat grams in 1 Big Mac (33 fat grams) vs the RDA (44 to 77 grams). This may help slow your urge. See bonus tip #4.

Bonus tip #2. Make sure you're getting enough magnesium in your diet. You can read about magnesium CLICK HERE.

Bonus tip #3. The color blue is proven to help curb your appetite. A blue screen saver or blue paint is often a good idea. There is a reason almost all fast food restaurants use the colors red, orange and yellow. They make you hungry.

Bonus tip #4. Get up, go outside for a brisk walk. This releases endorphins which suppress the desire to eat. It also helps take your mind off eating. It's also just a good idea. Instead of going for a second helping, go for a walk.

Remember, it all boils down to move more, eat less. Check out my blog on how we really lose fat.
