Health Benefits of Magnesium.

Positive Health Effects of Magnesium, The Super Mineral.



An estimated 80% of the human population is deficient in the essential mineral magnesium. Magnesium is involved in over 300 metabolic reactions. Magnesium is crucial in ATP (adenosine triphosphate) synthesis within the mitochondria of a cell . Magnesium benefits are associated with stroke and heart health, high blood pressure, asthma, bone and teeth health (osteoporosis), collagen production (healthy skin, hair), type 2 diabetes (improved insulin sensitivity), weight control (improved insulin sensitivity), fatigue (maintaining energy throughout the day), anxiety, muscle cramping, blood coagulation, headaches, sleep (muscle relaxation), constipation. Yeah, it's a super mineral. Many health experts say it is the most important dietary mineral. Too much of a good thing is not so good. RDA for men is +-400 mg and +-310 mg for women. The tolerable upper intake supplementation is 350 mg/day for adults. The Linus Pauling Institute is a good source of infomation on magnesium.

Good food sources of magnesium include spinach, brown rice, peas, avocados, oatmeal, broccoli, bananas, kidney beans, dark chocolate.
